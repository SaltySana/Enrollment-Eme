Academic Programs Overview

MSEUFCI offers comprehensive educational programs from Pre-Elementary to Senior High School, following the Philippine K-12 curriculum with additional specialized tracks. Our curriculum is designed to nurture holistic development, critical thinking, and prepare students for higher education and future careers.
